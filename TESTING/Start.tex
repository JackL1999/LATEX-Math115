The well known Pythagorean theorem \(x^2 + y^2 = z^2\) was 
proved to be invalid for other exponents. 
Meaning the next equation has no integer solutions:

\[ x^n + y^n = z^n \]
\\
\noindent
$2 \cdot 7 + 3 \cdot (2 - 12) + 6 \cdot 2$
\\
